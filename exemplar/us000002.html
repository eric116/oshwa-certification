Branding
Information

SparkFun’s logo and brand name are both protected by registered trademarks in the U.S. The “XBee Explorer Serial” product name, while not a registered trademark, is also protected by trademark law but to a lesser degree (because it is not registered) but it is still a unique product name. Their product tutorial is protected by copyright and licensed under a CC license. The red circuit board design may offer some trademark protection, because it is unique to Sparkfun and not necessary for the functioning of the product. The board’s schematic may receive some light protection in its color choices and use of logos and the Sparkfun brand name. But, because the circuit board itself and hardware on it are purely functional, they would likely receive no protection.
