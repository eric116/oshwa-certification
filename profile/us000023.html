The SHT15 Breakout is an easy to use, highly accurate, digital temperature and humidity sensor. The two sensors built into the SHT15 have been seamlessly coupled to a 14bit analog to digital converter and a serial interface circuit resulting in superior signal quality, fast response time, and a strong resistance to external disturbances.
